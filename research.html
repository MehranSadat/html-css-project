Afghan University’s Success Center is the first student-focused initiative of its kind in Afghanistan. The center assists our students and alumni to achieve personal and professional excellence. The center is a hub for academic and extra-curricular initiatives that aim to help our students and alumni become more efficient, effective and successful.

By partnering with faculty and other university offices, we ensure that our students and alumni excel both academically and professionally. Following are the different services offered through the Student Success Center:

  • Our Core Competencies & Strenghs
